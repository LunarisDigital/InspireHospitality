About Inspire Hospitality

An operator’s eye,
an owner’s conviction.

We have spent two decades opening and running hotels, training teams and convening the industry. Today that experience becomes the foundation for the places we build and own.

I — Our Story

We are building a platform that owns and operates the places we have always known how to build.

Since 2018, Inspire Hospitality has worked alongside companies, owners and investors to elevate guest experience and financial performance. The advisory practice built the reputation. Ownership is the next chapter.

From Advisor

The practice that earned the room.

Forty years of opening and running luxury hotels across six countries is not a credential — it is the operating system. We taught it, advised on it and refined it through training programs from China to Switzerland.

That depth is what owners, investors and educators have come to Inspire Hospitality for.

To Owner

Places begin with people.

Two decades of operational work taught us that hospitality is built before guests ever arrive — in the culture, the training and the team that will run a place.

Now we develop, acquire and steward boutique, wellness-led resorts on our own balance sheet, each one inheriting that operational depth.

II — Trajectory

From a practice
to a platform.

  1. 2018

    The advisory practice begins

    Inspire Hospitality is founded to help owners and investors elevate guest experience and financial performance.

  2. 2019

    Convening the industry

    The Inspire & Ignite Forum and the Women in Hospitality Forum bring operators, educators and partners together across China and beyond.

  3. 2020–22

    Education & cultural projects

    Hospitality-education initiatives deepen alongside cultural museum-hotel concepts and boutique resort development work.

  4. Today

    Owner & operator

    The platform evolves from advisor to owner-operator — developing, acquiring and stewarding wellness-led resorts with aligned capital partners.
